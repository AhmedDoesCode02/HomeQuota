Frequently Asked Questions

What is the maximum loan tenure in Egypt?
The Central Bank of Egypt currently allows maximum loan tenures of up to 25 years for residential properties.
Can foreigners obtain mortgages in Egypt?
Yes, many Egyptian banks offer mortgage products to foreign nationals with proper documentation.
